about

Fast Fr8 Logistics specialises in end-to-end transport and logistics solutions. From single vehicles to full fleets, we move assets safely and efficiently across regional and national routes. When something goes wrong, our rapid breakdown recovery keeps you moving, while our courier services handle urgent documents and time-critical parcels. Behind the scenes, advanced diagnostics and route planning help us maximise uptime, control costs, and deliver on time, every time.
